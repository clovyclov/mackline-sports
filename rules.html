RULES

How To Play

Mack Line Sports is a positive and fun online destination where you can participate in sports related trivia for free with the chance to win big prizes every week. You don't have to be a sports expert to participate in Mack Line Sports. All levels of sports knowledge are welcome to play!

Entry Rules

To get started, please read the Rules of Entry below. Thank you for participating, and have fun!

Only 1 entry per week per person will be allowed.

Entries must be submitted before the entry deadline.

Each entry will answer multiple questions about events that are expected to happen during the upcoming weekend.

The Results of each entry will be posted by noon, Central Standard Time, on the following Tuesday.

Tie Breaker

Closest to the tie-breaker answer, without going over! The tie breaker question will always require a numeric answer.

Earliest entry based on the entry submission time.
